About The Channel

I don't really remember when I made this channel, but the first video went up in February on 2019. I uploaded a few videos that year (most are privated now cause they are very bad), but I really started uploading around April of 2020. That run lasted until about mid-September, and had no consistency. I didn't really make any videos between then and about April 2021. I had a much shorter run of videos that time, only lasting about a month and a half. I basically didn't upload at all until I started making Let's Plays again (I'm hoping I don't fall into that hole again.) This time, I started putting videos out in late February 2023 and have been putting out at least 3 videos a week since.

About Me

My name is Jacob Moulton (I also go by Sy) and I'm 20 years old. I went to Delta College for 3 years and obtained an Associate's degree in Web Development. I plan to eventually resume my education and get a Bachelors' degree. I've been making videos on and off for over 4 years. After trying out a few different styles of content, I've settled into Let's Play content. I also occasionly vlog, but don't expect those too often.
